Hi there! My name is Gabriel De Cock, and I am a 23 year old fresh graduate from Boise State University. My Bachelor’s degree is in Games, Interactive Media and Mobile Technologies (GIMM), and during my studies I specialized in Game development and Design, as well as App Development, 3d modeling, and more. I have been passionate about video games, TTRPGs, and card games for my entire life. In the last 4 years, I’ve developed multiple short games, both through my degree and in my personal time. I believe that games and interactive media can help people, not just for entertainment but as tools to make the world a better place.
