I’m Gabe, a U.S., French, and German game developer and recent graduate from Boise State University, where I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM). I’ve lived in France, the U.S., and recently moved to Germany, which has shaped my collaborative approach and adaptability when working with diverse teams. I focus on game development and interactive storytelling - crafting experiences that combine creativity, empathy, and technology. I’ve always been fascinated by how games can do more than just entertain: they can teach, heal, and connect people in meaningful ways. Over the past few years, I’ve developed several small games and projects through both coursework and personal work. In my free time, I also enjoy role-playing games like D&D and card games like Magic: The Gathering, which fuel my passion for storytelling, strategy, and creative problem-solving and continue to inspire the worlds and systems I build in my own projects.



PROJECTS

GAME DEVELOPMENT

I designed this game in 2023, as an entry into the GMTK 2023 Game Jam; while I ultimately did not submit it to the competition, I want to share it here as an example of some of my earliest independent work.

I designed this VR game fully from scratch, as part of my degree. Everything, from the code, to the assets, to the sound effects, was created independently. The game functions as a simulation of a morning routine, and is intended to help people who may struggle with forming habits, such as people with learning disabilities.
